Connecting Talent with Opportunity

Career Development Centre @NUV

Navrachana University has always remained a preferred destination to hire fresh talent because of its highly professional student quality, industry-aligned curriculum, highly qualified & experienced faculties, Research facilities and strong industry connections.

Navrachana University’s placement process is designed to make recruitment easy and flexible to optimize students’ success. We help students find the right opportunities based on their interests and aptitude and by matching them with recruiting organizations’ requirements.

A thoughtfully designed and carefully nurtured vibrant campus culture ensures that students turn into well-rounded and sought-after professionals, while experiencing some of the best times of their lives.

With more than 500+ national and multinational recruiters on board, NUVites have plenty to choose from, if they don’t become job creators themselves. So, WHEN YOU ARE AT NUV, YOU ARE #WellPlaced

Our commitment to nurturing talent and providing the best opportunities ensures that every student finds the right path to success, whether in the corporate world or as an entrepreneur.

Placement Process

Step 1: Training, Expert Talks, Placement Orientation
Step 2: Placement Cell Invite Companies for Campus Placements
Step 3: Placement Cell shares opportunities with students
Step 4: Interested students apply for the opportunity
Step 5: Pre-Placement Talk and selection process
Step 6: Selection process based on:
  • Aptitude Test
  • Psychometric Test
  • Group Discussion
  • Personal Interview
Step 7: Final Selection & Offer Confirmation
